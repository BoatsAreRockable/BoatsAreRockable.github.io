## Features

Boats Animator is an open source frame capture program that has been created to provide a powerful and easy to use alternative to the ageing existing free programs. Its intuitive interface caters to animators of all levels and offers many powerful features.

The program has been open sourced under the GNU General Public License and contributions are welcome to the project&#8217;s active <a href="https://github.com/boatsarerockable/animator" target="_blank">GitHub repository</a>.

        Instantly preview your captured frames with Boats Animator. Unlike other programs, there is no need to wait for the frames to process.</td>

            Onion Skinning
